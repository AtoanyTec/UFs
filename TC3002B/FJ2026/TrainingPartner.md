# 🏥 Dermatoss — Medical Vanguard

## 👩‍⚕️ Who are they?
**Dermatoss Vanguardia Médica** is a specialized dermatology clinic focused on comprehensive care of the skin, hair, and nails for patients of all ages (children and adults).

Link: https://www.dermatoss.com/

---

## 👩‍⚕️ Medical leadership
The clinic is led by **Dr. Patricia Sepúlveda**, who has:

- Over **25 years of experience**
- Medical training from **UNAM (National Autonomous University of Mexico)**
- Specialization in **pediatric dermatology**
- Experience at institutions such as the **Hospital Infantil de México**

---

## 🧪 Main services

Dermatoss provides comprehensive dermatological care, including:

- Treatment of **acne, psoriasis, and vitiligo**
- Diagnosis and screening for **skin cancer**
- **Pediatric dermatology**
- Treatment of **hair and scalp conditions** (e.g., alopecia)
- Procedures such as:
  - Cryotherapy
  - Laser treatments
  - Lesion removal
- Nail disease treatment
